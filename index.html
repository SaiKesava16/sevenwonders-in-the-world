About Seven Wonders:

The Seven Wonders of the World are renowned landmarks, symbolizing human creativity, culture, and historical importance. Originally, the term referred to the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, a list from antiquity that celebrated some of the most remarkable architectural and artistic achievements of the time.

The seven Wonders in the world are shown below:

  • The Great Wall of China
  • The taj Mahal
  • Manchu Picchu
  • The Pyramids Of Giza
  • The Roman Colosseum
  • The Christ The Redeemer Statue
  • Chichen Itza
